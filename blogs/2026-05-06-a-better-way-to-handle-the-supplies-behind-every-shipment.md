Shipping workflows have been streamlined, but supply sourcing still sits outside the flow—creating small interruptions that slow teams down over time. This article explores how bringing supplies into the same platform as fulfillment removes that friction, helping teams stay focused and keep orders moving. By connecting one more step in the process, businesses can create a more cohesive, efficient operation where everything happens in one place.
